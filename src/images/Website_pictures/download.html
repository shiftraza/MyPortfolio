Pella Windows

Scottish Home Improvements is proud to partner with Pella, one of the largest window manufacturers in the country, to provide homeowners in Denver with premium replacement windows. We prefer to use Pella windows on most of our installs due to their superior performance and beauty. Pella windows offer sophisticated style, exceptional energy efficiency, and design flexibility. And to top it off, all Pella windows are warp and fade resistant and come with a limited lifetime warranty. Pella windows are available with a variety of different frames, including wood, fiberglass, and vinyl, as well as any window style of your choice.

Andersen Windows

Anderson is one of the longest existing window manufacturers located in North America. Anderson has been creating replacement windows since 1903 and has had decades to perfect the design and performance of their products. Anderson offers a variety of collections and customization options so that you can get the exact look and feel you desire for your home renovations. Anderson windows reduce noise, drafts, and protect your home from the elements, giving you both comfort and peace of mind. Furthermore, their sustainable manufacturing processes makes their windows an eco friendly solution for your home.

Alside Windows

A long established industry leader, Alside is equipped with over 65 years of experience in window design and construction. Made from vinyl resin reinforced with stabilizers and impact modifiers, Alside windows offer both a stunning appearance and energy efficiency and are specially engineered to be easy to open and operate. At Scottish Home Improvements, we carry  Alside’s entire product line of premium windows, including Sheffield, UltraMaxx, Mezzo, and Fusion models, each of which can be completely customize to ensure that it fits perfectly and looks beautiful in your home.
